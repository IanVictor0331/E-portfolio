  <h1>Video Blog: UTM Digital Open Day</h1>
  <p>A vlog documenting the industrial visit to UTM Digital, featuring interviews with staff and summaries of technical talks regarding university-wide digital infrastructure.</p>

  <h2>Talks & Key Takeaways</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Digital Services & Accessibility:</strong> 
      Mr. Razi bin Muhammad explained the various services created for UTM staff and students, such as <strong>UTM Smart</strong> and <strong>MyUTM</strong>, which centralize campus information for easy access.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Data Center Insights:</strong> 
      Mr. Hafi provided a detailed look into the UTM Digital Data Center, discussing the "Tier List" rankings and the technical requirements for maintaining a large-scale database center.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>The Role of Data Engineering:</strong> 
      Mr. Zahari shared insights into why data engineering is critical, explaining how data is processed and managed within an actual organization.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Interview Highlights</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Emerging Technologies:</strong> 
      Staff confirmed that UTM Digital is currently developing projects involving <strong>AI</strong> and <strong>Blockchain</strong>, specifically for verifying student certificates.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Problem Solving:</strong> 
      The team uses weekly "stand-up meetings" to discuss technical challenges and brainstorm solutions to ensure system reliability.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Successful Projects:</strong> 
      Recent successes include the integration of the Research and Development system and various internal web portals.
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Reflections</h2>
  <p>The visit helped bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application. Seeing how a large university with thousands of students manages its digital services has provided a clearer vision of future careers in data engineering.</p>
