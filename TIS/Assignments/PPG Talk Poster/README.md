  <h1>Industry Talk: PPG - Innovation & Data Management</h1>
  <p>This report documents an industry talk presented by PPG, a global leader in the coatings industry, focusing on their specialized systems and the role of data engineering in corporate innovation.</p>

  <hr>

  <h2>Key Technologies & Systems</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <strong>Global Marine Hull Navigator:</strong> 
      A predictive system designed to improve ship performance by analyzing sea conditions and tracking data to deliver fouling risk algorithms.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Supply Chain Control Tower:</strong> 
      A high-tech digital hub used to manage and analyze the complexities of a global supply chain from a single platform.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>SAP Implementation:</strong> 
      Utilization of SAP software across three modules—Functional, Technical, and Reporting—to manage all corporate resources.
    </li>
    <li>
      <strong>Data Analytics Stack:</strong> 
      Insights into the tools used by their Data Engineering team, including Power BI, Delta Lake, Azure Data Lake, and Scala.
    </li>
  </ul>
